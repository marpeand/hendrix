Gamify Your Life transforms boring routines into an exciting game. Users can:

- Earn points for completing tasks like exercising, studying, or working.
- Unlock achievements and climb leaderboards.
- Set long-term goals and track progress visually.

### Core Features

1. **Task Manager**: Integrate with calendars to sync tasks.
2. **Reward System**: Customize rewards to suit personal preferences.
3. **Social Features**: Compete with friends or join teams for collaborative goals.

By making productivity fun, this app motivates users to stay consistent and achieve their goals.
